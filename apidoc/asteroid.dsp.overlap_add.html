class asteroid.dsp.overlap_add.DualPathProcessing(chunk_size, hop_size)[source]¶

Bases: torch.nn.Module

Perform Dual-Path processing via overlap-add as in DPRNN [1].

Args:

chunk_size (int): Size of segmenting window. hop_size (int): segmentation hop size.

References

[1] “Dual-path RNN: efficient long sequence modeling for

time-domain single-channel speech separation”, Yi Luo, Zhuo Chen and Takuya Yoshioka. https://arxiv.org/abs/1910.06379

asteroid.dsp.overlap_add._reorder_sources(current: torch.FloatTensor, previous: torch.FloatTensor, n_src: int, window_size: int, hop_size: int)[source]¶

Reorder sources in current chunk to maximize correlation with previous chunk. Used for Continuous Source Separation. Standard dsp correlation is used for reordering.

Parameters

  • current (torch.Tensor) – current chunk, tensor of shape (batch, n_src, window_size)

  • previous (torch.Tensor) – previous chunk, tensor of shape (batch, n_src, window_size)

  • n_src (int) – number of sources.

  • window_size (int) – window_size, equal to last dimension of both current and previous.

  • hop_size (int) – hop_size between current and previous tensors.
